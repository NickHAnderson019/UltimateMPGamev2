Instructions

Skill Budget: Choose your player's skills. The sum must be less than 12.

Movement: Use the WASD keys to move your player. Hold shift to sprint. You can only sprint if you have energy (bottom left)

Throwing: Click to throw. The disc will go to the position of your mouse pointer. To curve the disc, use the A (left-to-right) and D (right-to-left) keys. Hold W to throw the disc hard.

Catching: Just run into the path of the disc
